What do you mean by REST API?

RESTful APIs are widely used in web development and are the basis for many web services and web APIs, including those used in mobile app development, web applications, and other distributed systems. They provide a simple and standardized way for clients to interact with server resources over the HTTP protocol, making them highly interoperable and scalable.
